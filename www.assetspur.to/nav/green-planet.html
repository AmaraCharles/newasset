We Join Forces to Impact Our Future, and Together We Are Making a Difference

Unbelievable! More than 200,000 acres of rainforest are burned every day. That is more than 150 acres lost every minute of every day, and 78 million acres lost every year!

More than 20 percent of the Amazon rainforest is already gone, and much more is severely threatened as the destruction continues.

Why our work is good for everyone involved?

Personal investing in top-quality raw materials with a global demand? Earning money without harming man nor nature? Is this even possible? We are convinced there is no other long-term solution. Therefore we made sustainability our main objective and foundation of the assetspur model. The result is a real win-win situation.

Earn and help our planet.

Each assetspur DeFi Hybrid Contract helps to generate more profits.
Together we spend 2% ( 1% assetspur / 1% from You ) to plant trees.

We understand the need for full disclosure and transparency hence all assetspur Hybrid Contract holders will be notified 24 hours before a token DONATION will take place.

This allows full transparency towards donations, and all holders can view the allocated wallet to see whom and what was paid for at all times and complete amounts spent, all receipts will then be automatically uploaded to our website from the designated charity/reforestation foundation.

100% transparancy means everyone in the assetspur community can control this 100% themselves!
