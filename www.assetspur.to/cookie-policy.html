USE OF COOKIES

We may record your visit to this site for analysing the number of visitors to this site, general usage patterns and your personal usage patterns and improving your experience. We may gather some of this information through the use of “cookies”.

WHAT IS COOKIE?

Cookies are small bits of information that are automatically stored on your web browser in your computer, mobile phone or other device for accessing the internet that can be retrieved by this site. We will be able to access the information stored on the cookies and record how you use this site.

WHY DO YOU USE COOKIES?

Cookies are useful because they allow us to recognise your device and they store information about your use of this site/app which enables us to provide more useful features to you, to tailor the content of our website and mobile applications to suit your interests and, where permitted by your marketing preferences, provide you with promotional materials or direct marketing based on your usage patterns.

WHAT IF I DON’T WANT YOU TO DO THIS?

Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. If you prefer, you can set your browser to disable cookies – you can do this through your browser setting (your browser’s ‘help’ function should tell you how to do this).
By disabling cookies, you may not be able to take full advantage of this site, including assetspur Wallet Account.
If you accept cookies during your use of this site or continue to use this app, you will be acknowledging that your information is being collected, stored, accessed and used as outlined above.

MANAGE COOKIES

You can disable the use of cookies in your web browser or delete cookies in your web browser settings. However, this would mean that our website and some functions of our service may not function properly.
The management of cookies can usually be found in the “Settings”, “Preferences” or “Option” menu of your web browser.
